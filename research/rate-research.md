# City of Spokane Solid Waste Utility
The City's solid waste enterprise services are comprised of collection and disposal services.

## Collection
The Collection Department is responsible for providing residential and commercial collection services.

### Residential Collection
There are approximately 72,000 residential customers in the City of Spokane. Residential customers can get the following services:

#### 1. Garbage
- Mandatory weekly service
- Rates based on cart type:
    - 30-gallon cart: current = $23.22/month
    - 60-gallon cart: current = $42.04/month
    - 90-gallon cart: current = $61.39/month
- Approximately 62,000 tons per year
- City provides cart to customer at City's expense

#### 2. Yard Debris
- Optional service
    - Winter months (Jan, Feb, Dec): monthly service (first week)
    - Non-winter months (all others): weekly service
- Rates based on garbage cart size selected above
- Approximately 20,000 tons per year
- City provides cart to customer at City's expense

#### 3. Recycling
- Every-other-week service
- Rates based on garbage cart size selected above
- Apprixmately 20,000 tons per year
- City provides cart to customer at City's expense

### Commercial Collection
Commercial customers can get garbage and recycling services. Commercial garbage tonnage runs about 65,000 tons per year.  For garbage, services include:

#### 1. Small Dumpsters
- Mandatory weekly service
- Rates based on dumpster type and # weekly pickups:
    - 1-yard dumpster: current = $124.63/month
    - 2-yard dumpster: current = $249.25/month
    - 3-yard dumpster: current = $373.90/month
    - 4-yard dumpster: current = $498.53/month
    - 6-yard dumpster: current = $747.78/month
- Customers needing more than 1x pickup per week: multiply rate above by # weekly pickups
- City provides dumpster to customer at City's expense

#### 2. Roll-Off Dumpsters
- Optional service
- Rates based on # of pickups: current = $177.59/pickup
- Note: there is also a weight-based charge incurred at the Disposal facility; these per-ton rates are described below under Disposal
- City provides roll-off to customer at City's expense

#### 3. Small Compactors
- Optional service
- Rates based on compactor type and # weekly pickups:
    - 1-yard compactor: current = $240.85/month
    - 2-yard compactor: current = $481.71/month
    - 3-yard compactor: current = $722.55/month
    - 4-yard compactor: current = $963.41/month
    - 5-yard compactor: current = $1,204.25/month
    - 6-yard compactor: current = $1,445.09/month
- Customers needing more than 1x pickup per week: multiply rate above by # weekly pickups
- Customer provides compactor at their own expense

#### 4. Tube Compactors
- Optional service
- Rates based on # of pickups: current = $210.13/pickup
- Note: there is also a weight-based charge incurred at the Disposal facility; these per-ton rates are described below under Disposal
- Customer provides compactor at their own expense

## Disposal
The Disposal Department provides waste disposal services through the operation of a Waste-to-Energy (WTE) facility that accepts garbage, yard debris, recycling and household hazardous wastes (HHW). Recycling and HHW are provided to the public in a drop-off area at the WTE facility FREE of charge.

### Garbage Disposal
- Rates based on weight: current = $141.90/ton
- Minimum charge: $23.19 for up to 320 pounds + $2.49 transaction fee per vehicle

### Yard debris
- Rates based on weight: current = $71.56/ton
- Minimum charge: $7.62 for up to 200 pounds + $2.49 transaction fee per vehicle
